Senior Statistical Officer

International Research Organization

Location: Mohakhali, Dhaka Salary: 83,820 BDT per Month Full-Time

Job Summary

We are seeking a highly skilled Statistical Officer to lead data management and analysis for a large-scale community-based trial. The ideal candidate will have extensive experience in longitudinal data analysis and public health research.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform advanced statistical analysis using R and Stata.
  • Clean and manage large datasets from field surveys.
  • Prepare technical reports and contribute to manuscript writing.
  • Ensure data quality and integrity according to international protocols.

Requirements

  • Masters in Biostatistics, Applied Statistics, or Public Health.
  • Minimum 3 years of experience in a research setting (icddr,b experience preferred).
  • Proficiency in R, Stata, and LaTeX.
